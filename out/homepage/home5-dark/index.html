Plowave DAO is an exclusive DAO community of 99 Aptos Guardians with the aim of supporting early-stage projects built on Aptos to further the expansion and growth of the Aptos Ecosystem.


Organization

How is Plowave structured?

Structure

Plowave uses Aptos multisig wallet to manage its fund and smart contracts to deploy capital to early-stage projects built on Crypto and proceeds and dividends back to the Members.

For-Profit

Plowave is a for-profit organization of which Members will receive proceeds and dividends on a pro-rata basis based on the number of warrant tokens each Member holds.

Investments

Using the "hive mind" of a DAO, the Members will collectively vote and deploy early-stage investments in projects that aim to be the next mass adoption.

Operations

The terms and provisions of Plowave may be amended if 50% or more of Plowave Members or their delegates (as measured by the warrant tokens) vote to approve an amendment.

Aptos. Guardians. Plowave.

Members of Plowave are individuals who have demonstrated significant contributions to the Aptos community as founders, builders, engineers, community leaders, product managers, and investors. Over time, Plowave could evolve in a number of different directions as the Members have the right to vote on every matter.
